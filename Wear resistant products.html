Wear Resistant Products

For power, steel, aluminum, and cement sectors, abrasive materials are pneumatically 

or hydraulically conveyed through pipelines resulting in heavy wear and tear. This 

calls for the use of Protective liner material in the medium.

The advantage of using Ceracoat and Derincoat liners is that it provides

a monolithic lined surface without any edge effects and thus ensures a smooth and 

uninterrupted flow of material without causing damage to the medium. Ensuring durability, reliability and efficiency.
