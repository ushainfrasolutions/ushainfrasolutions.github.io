Nepal Project

Usha Infra Solutions carried out setup of an Extrusion Line Manufacturing facility based in Nepal. UISPL was involved right from the feasibility and planning of project to testing of extruded component.

• Excavation work for underground Piping      system of Extrusion Plant
• Ensures absolute system planning and factory room utilization.
• Factory shade ensuring structural integrity, roof bracings, natural light and ventilation. 

The 12000 sqft. project was completed in stages within a period of 12 months. The supply of extruders, capacitors and other necessary units was possible with our wide network of manufacturers and expertise in project solutions.
