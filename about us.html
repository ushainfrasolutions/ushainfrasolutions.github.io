Overview

We at Usha Infra Solutions Pvt Ltd are a multi-disciplinary organization with primary focus on Engineering Exports from India. We are an organisation with diversified interest in, Steel plants, Power plants, Cement plants, Sugar industries and other Process Industries providing a wide range of Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC), Operation & Maintenance Services and Products including Balance of Plant packages, Materials , spareparts, retrofits. Usha Infra Solutions Pvt Ltd , since its inception , by virtue of transparent, universal, progressive, and timeless values has emerged as a leading private sector organization in India. We have a portfolio in system integration of engineering projects and are fully equipped to undertake Engineering, Procurement for various infrastructure projects.
